Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Pikakard ("the app") is a card-drawing tool for tarot, playing cards, and flash cards. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app does and does not collect.

What we collect

Pikakard does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. The app has no accounts, no login, and no analytics.

Local storage

The app saves your current session (which cards are in play, your deck and draw settings) to your device's local storage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser or app storage

Third-party services

Pikakard does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services.

Children's privacy

Pikakard does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. Because we collect no data at all, the app is safe for all ages.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change our data practices, we will update this page and revise the "Last updated" date above.
