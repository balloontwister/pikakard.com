Features

Everything you need for a card reading, nothing you don't.

🃏

Multiple Decks

Switch between Rider-Waite-Smith Tarot, Marseille Tarot, and a standard 52-card playing card deck — with more on the way.

🔀

Flexible Deals

Draw 1 card or a full spread. Choose face-up, face-down, or mixed. Finite draws or infinite cycling — you set the rules.

📖

Card Details

Tap any card to see its name, keywords, and description. Swipe through the full spread without closing the detail view.

📲

Works Offline

Install Pikakard as a home-screen app on any device. No internet required after first load. Your session is saved locally.

Available Decks

Carefully sourced and formatted for a great reading experience.

Rider-Waite-Smith Tarot

The iconic 78-card deck illustrated by Pamela Colman Smith in 1909. Public domain. The standard reference deck for modern tarot study and reading.

Marseille Tarot

A faithful reproduction of Nicholas Conver's 1760 Marseille deck, restored and released to the community by Yoav Ben Dov. One of the oldest surviving tarot traditions.

Playing Cards

A standard 52-card deck. Use it for cartomancy, card games, or everyday draws. The same familiar deck in your pocket.

Coming Soon

Flash Cards

Create your own flash card decks for studying, language learning, or any subject. Custom decks with your content.
